    Oh, joy of joys, another Monday’s here, 
    The thrill it brings is absolutely clear.
    The alarm clock’s scream, a symphony so sweet,
    I leap from bed with such light, nimble feet.

    The traffic jam, a glorious parade,
    With honking horns, my patience starts to fade.
    Oh, coffee stains, my shirt’s new, stylish flair,
    A fashion statement, not just disrepair.

    Emails flood in with urgent, vital tasks,
    Responding fast, oh, how the time just flies!
    The boss’s praise, so rare and so sincere,
    Like spotting unicorns or finding cheer.

    Oh, lunchtime comes, a feast of bland delight,
    A soggy sandwich, truly out of sight.
    And after hours, joy that never ends,
    With TV shows and chores as loyal friends.

    To bed I go, with dreams of more routine,
    For life’s a thrilling, vibrant, wondrous scene.
    Oh, mundane life, you’re truly such a bore,
    But what’s the point of wanting something more?
    

    by - Snooty McSnark
